About Us

IRON'EFREI was created as a passion project by three students with a shared love for sport, technology, and great design : Axel STOLZ, Thibeau CHESNEL and Enzo GONZALEZ. Meet the team behind the site:

William ROBERT

Role : HTML structure, design direction, and front-end feature implementation.

I Handled the architecture and layout of every page, ensuring the user experience is intuitive and visually engaging. My focus was on translating creative vision into responsive, functional web components.

Victor TAILLEFER

Role : Content researcher, CSS styling, and technical enhancements.

On my side I led the content generation effort—researching triathlon-specific information to ensure everything was accurate and useful. I also fine-tuned the CSS for each page and contributed greatly to the visual consistency of the site.

Collaborative Work

Together, we brainstormed and developed interactive features such as JSON-based training plans, countdown timers, and other dynamic elements that made this project more than just a static site.

Inspiring strength, endurance, and unity through triathlons.
