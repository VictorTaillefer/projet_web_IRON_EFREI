About Us

IRON'EFREI was created as a passion project by three students with a shared love for sport, technology, and great design : Axel STOLZ, Thibeau CHESNEL and Enzo GONZALEZ. Meet the team behind the site:

Meet the team

William ROBERT

Role : HTML structure, design direction, and front-end feature implementation.

I worked on the structure and styling of multiple pages, focusing on clean HTML and responsive CSS. I helped plan the site layout and navigation flow to ensure it stayed intuitive across devices. I also collaborated closely on JavaScript features like training plans and interactivity, and contributed to refining the overall visual direction. Each page was a joint effort in terms of review and iteration, even when the implementation was split.

Victor TAILLEFER
