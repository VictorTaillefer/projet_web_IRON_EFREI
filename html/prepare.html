Triathlon Training

Training for a triathlon is a test of consistency, endurance, and discipline. It involves carefully balancing swim, bike, and run workouts while allowing time for recovery and proper fueling. Whether you're training for a sprint or an Ironman, having a structured plan is key to achieving your goals without burnout or injury.

Recommended Training Time & Finish Goals

Distance Recommended Training Time (weeks) Average Finishing Time
Sprint 6–8 weeks 1h 15m – 1h 45m
Olympic 8–12 weeks 2h 30m – 3h 30m
70.3 (Half Ironman) 12–20 weeks 5h 30m – 7h 30m
140.6 (Full Ironman) 20–30 weeks 11h – 15h
