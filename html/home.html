What is a Triathlon?

A triathlon is a multisport race that combines swimming, cycling, and running into a single grueling competition. It's a test of endurance, strength, and willpower, attracting athletes from all walks of life.

IRON'EFREI is a club that we made here at Efrei with hopes of gathering likeminded that share a deep passion for sports.

Triathlon Distances

Sprint Distance: Maximum effort, minimum distance.
This distance is considered one of the hardest, having to go all out for the entire race.
Distance :
750m swim
20km bike
5km run

Olympic Distance: Maximum effort, extended distance. Requires a sustained high effort across all three disciplines, demanding both speed and endurance.
Distance:
1.5km swim
40km bike
10km run

Half Ironman: Maximum effort, extended distance. Requires a sustained high effort across all three disciplines, demanding both speed and endurance.
Distance:
1.5km swim
40km bike
10km run

Full Ironman: Extreme endurance, mental fortitude. The ultimate test of endurance, pushing athletes to their physical and mental limits for an entire day
Distance:
3.8km swim
180km bike
42.2km run
