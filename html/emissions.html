Triathlon & the Planet

Endurance sports thrive in nature. Let's protect the playground we race in.

What's the Impact?

Travel Emissions

Over 60% of an event's carbon footprint comes from athlete and spectator travel.

Waste Generation

Plastic cups, wrappers, signs — races generate tons of landfill waste annually.

Resource Use

From bottled water to single-use bibs, triathlons often use more than they need.

Greener Racing Starts With You

  • Carpool or train it: Travel smarter and cut emissions dramatically.
  • Bring a reusable bottle: Skip the disposable cups.
  • Support green events: Look for certified sustainable races.
  • Offset your carbon: Contribute to reforestation or clean energy projects.

Join the Movement

We're building a community of conscious athletes. Share your own race habits and inspire others to go greener.
